The SIES Journal of Humanities is a peer-reviewed, double-blind multidisciplinary annual online research journal (ISSN: 3048-9768 Online). It focuses on interdisciplinary perspectives in social sciences and promotes quality research globally.

The journal aims to continue the institution's legacy of fostering a research culture, ensuring sustainable development, and publishing world-class articles that open new windows to humanities.

All submissions undergo a double-blind peer-review process to ensure academic rigor and quality. Authors receive feedback within 8-10 weeks of submission.

Manuscripts can be submitted via email to siesjournalofhumanities@gmail.com. Ensure your submission follows the journal's guidelines, including APA 7th edition formatting and a cover letter with author details.
